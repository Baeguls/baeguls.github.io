June 8
On Living in NYC
I think New York is the greatest city on earth because it is the most involved city on Earth. Living here is like having a really hot, really high maintenance girlfriend. Shes wants all your money and she’s going to make you wait 2 hours for her to get ready, but when you get to make love to her, oh my god— you know what they say about New York nines? In the suburbs, where I grew up, everything is frictionless. You can drive to Costco, you don’t have to talk to anyone all day, you always have air conditioning, and here, it smells like piss. But EVERY SO OFTEN, you can really make love to this place. Like, LORDE WILL JUST RANDOMLY SHOW UP IN THE PARK AND PERFORM.
That’s New York, man. It won't happen anywhere else.
